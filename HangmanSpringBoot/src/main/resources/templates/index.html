Welcome to Hangman

A classic word-guessing game. Pick a word list and start guessing!

How to Play

  • A secret word is chosen at random.
  • Click a letter button to guess it.
  • A correct guess reveals that letter in the word.
  • An incorrect guess adds a body part to the gallows.
  • You have 8 incorrect guesses before the game is lost.
  • Reveal all letters before the man is hanged to win!
